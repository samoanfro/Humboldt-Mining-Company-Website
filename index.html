A Documented Nevada Project

Advancing the Prunty Mine on the foundation of independent sampling, metallurgy, and compliance.

The Prunty Mine has been worked intermittently since 1905 for gold, silver, copper, and antimony. Humboldt Mining's current program is focused on selective exposure of mineralized vein material, systematic sampling supported by accredited laboratories, metallurgical testing, and a defined commercial pathway for ore.

Three Pillars

What's supporting the project today.

— 01

Independent sampling

Channel, grab, and bulk samples of vein, adit, and historic dump material, verified by accredited independent laboratories with umpire cross-checks on selected samples.

— 02

Documented metallurgy

Bulk samples submitted to an independent metallurgical laboratory have demonstrated amenability to standard gravity concentration and cyanidation processing methods on the material tested.

— 03

Permitted and inspected

Most recent NDEP-BMRR compliance inspection (August 2025) closed with no items of concern. Active MSHA mine ID and written HazCom program in place.
